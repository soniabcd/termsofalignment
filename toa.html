Terms of Alignment Worksheet

An interactive guide to defining your professional terms for sustainable success and well-being.

I. The Purpose Statement

Start by defining your 'why'. This is your anchor. Why did you choose this role? What is the core intention behind this professional chapter?

Guiding Questions

  • What growth opportunity excites me most?
  • What am I consciously trading for this?
  • How will this role help me become who I want to be?
  • What lessons from the past will I apply now?

Your Purpose

II. Balancing the Scales: Trade-Offs vs. Returns

Every role involves a balance. Being honest about the sacrifices and clear on the expected benefits is crucial for long-term satisfaction.

Acknowledged Trade-Offs

List the known costs of your commitment. Assign an impact score from 1-10.

Expected Returns

List the tangible and intangible benefits you expect, with a value score from 1-10.

III. Your Commitment & Your Boundaries

Success requires a strong commitment and firm boundaries. This is your pledge.

My Professional Pledge

Define what you will bring to your work. This is your professional promise.

My Non-Negotiables

Define your boundaries. These are the pillars of your well-being that are not up for sacrifice.

    IV. From Strategy to Tactics

    Your non-negotiables are protected by concrete actions. Define the specific tactics you will use to maintain balance and manage your energy effectively.

    Define Your Tactics

    V. The Journey: Regular Checkpoints

    Alignment is an ongoing process. Schedule regular check-ins to assess your journey, recalibrate, and ensure your contract is being met.

    1M

    Month 1: First Impressions

    Compare the role to your expectations. How is the pace? Is the initial excitement still there? What needs immediate adjustment?

    3M

    Month 3: Deeper Reality

    Are you learning what you came to learn? Is the cost in time and energy manageable? Do you still feel like yourself?

    6M

    Month 6: Value & Visibility

    Do you feel valued and visible? Have the gains justified the trade-offs? Are you thriving or just surviving?

    12M

    Month 12: The Long View

    Have you built tangible skills and assets? Do you have more career leverage? Is it time to renew your commitment or plan your next move?
